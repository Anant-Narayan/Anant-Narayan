JEE Apex Board: JEE Apex Board got the responsibility of JEE Mains and JEE Advanced exam, know the functions of the new board

JEE Mains, JEE Advanced exam will now be conducted by JEE Apex Board, not NTA. The Ministry of Education has issued a notice in this regard. 19-member JEE Apex Board for the examination of JEE to be held for admission in engineering institutes other than IITs, NITs in the year 2022 and 2023. (JEE Apex Board) has constituted. JEE Mains Phase I examination will be conducted by this new board from June 20. The Ministry of Education has said in the notice that JEE Main and Advanced examination (JEE Advanced Exam) JEE Apex Board has been constituted for the smooth conduct of the examination. The new board will be headed by Professor Bhaskar Ramamurthy, former director of IIT Madras.

As per the notification, JEE Apex Board (JAB) JAB will have a permanent secretariat, which will be provided by the National Agency. JAB will have the authority to decide the policies and rules regarding the conduct of JEE Main examination, which will also coordinate with the JEE Advanced Group and its Chairman. The Ministry of Education has constituted JAB to make the system of JEE Main and JEE Advanced more transparent and better. The tenure of the previous board ended on 31 March 2022.

members of this board

Directors of IIT Kharagpur, IIT Bangalore, IIT Bombay, IIT Jaipur, IIT Rourkela, IIT Nagpur, IIT Guwahati, and IIT DM Kurnool will attend. Other members of the board include representatives from Gujarat, Haryana, Karnataka and Bihar, representatives of SRM Institute of Science and Technology, Chairman of CBSE, Director General of NIC, Director General of C-DAC, Additional Secretary/Joint Secretary in the Ministry of Education and National Examination Agency. Director General will be involved.

JEE exam

JEE Main 2022 session 1 examinations will now be conducted from June 20 to 29, 2022. The same session 2 exam will be held from 21 to 30 July 2022. JEE Advanced exam will be conducted on 28 August 2022. The registration process for JEE Advanced 2022 will start from 7th August and will end on 11th August. Eligible candidates can register for this exam from 7th August onwards.
